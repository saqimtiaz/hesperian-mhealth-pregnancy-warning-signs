Chemicals and fumes

Chemicals used for cleaning and to kill pests are very dangerous to pregnant women. A pregnant woman should avoid getting chemicals on her skin or breathing their fumes. Chemical containers should never be used to carry or store food or water.

Chemicals can cause:

  • Miscarriage - pregnancy ends too soon and baby dies
  • Infertility - cannot become pregnant
  • Birth defects - harm to baby in the womb
  • Cancer and other illnesses

Poisonous chemicals are found in:

  • Pesticides and fertilizers used in farming
  • Pesticides to kill insects or rodents
  • Cleaners, solvents, and plastics used at home or a factory where she works
  • Some glue and paints
  • Gasoline, oil, and other fuels
